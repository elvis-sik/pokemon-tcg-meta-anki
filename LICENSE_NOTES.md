# Licensing and attribution notes

The handoff contains original schemas, scripts, templates, and derived
set-number/archetype mappings. It does not redistribute card images or bulk
card text.

Data and links refer to:

- Limitless TCG for tournament/deck-list locators and metagame pages.
- TCGdex for structured card and set data during the local build.

TCGdex's software/database project publishes its own licensing terms. Pokémon
card images, artwork, names, logos, and rules text remain subject to the rights
of The Pokémon Company and other applicable rights holders. The coding agent
should review source terms before distributing a finished deck publicly.
